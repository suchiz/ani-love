QUEL EST NOTRE CONCEPT ?

Véritables amoureux des animaux, nous avons choisi d’allier passion et métier en ouvrant en 2015, votre magasin spécialisé en animaux de compagnie, Animal Protect. Très engagés dans la protection animale et le respect du vivant, notre nouveau concept d’animalerie sans animaux en vente reflète notre éthique et notre état d’esprit.
C'est également pour cela que, dans notre magasin, vous trouverez des croquettes naturelles avec des viandes d’excellentes qualités répondant aux mieux aux besoins primaires de vos chiens et chats mais aussi de nombreuses friandises pour chiens et chats, jouets, accessoires axés 100% sur le bien-être et le respect de leurs besoins.
Nous voulons que chiens, chats, chiots, chatons et maintenant NACS (lapins, cochons d’Indes, hamsters, furets…) trouvent leurs bonheurs avec des produits adaptés au plus près de leurs besoins.

De plus, nous sommes partenaires avec des associations de protection animale sur Toulouse et sa région et luttons activement au quotidien pour leur venir en aide.
En tant que clients Animal Protect, vous pouvez devenir acteur pour le sauvetage et la prise en charge d’animaux abandonnés ou dans le besoin, en adhérant à notre association partenaire. Vous bénéficierez en plus de remises immédiates.

Nous serons heureux de vous accueillir dans votre animalerie toulousaine nouvelle génération, basée sur le conseil et la diversité de produits. Toujours dans le bien-être animal et la recherche constante de votre satisfaction.

A très bientôt !

Qui suis-je ?

I am Julien Hongsavanh, the Suchiz Corp and Suchiz Games founder. I am 27 years old. I am specialized in the field of image processing and computer vision, I advise companies and individuals to implement their projects. I mainly work on innovative projects involving artificial intelligence such as object detection, recognition and segmentation on video streams or point clouds but it also include pre-processing such as image enhancement etc...

No matter the sector of your activity, there is always room for innovation !

I have been passionate about computers since I was a child. It started with video games, then at the age of 13, I programmed my first game in C. At that age, I was already convinced that I would work in computer science later on. Then, it's only in my second year of bachelor's degree that I am fascinated by digital imaging.

Indeed, digital imaging is the field in which I would like to work because it is omnipresent in everyday life and its needs are increasing while being different from day to day. It is this fascinating and challenging diversity that makes this field the most attractive to me. For instance for image processing, there is Big Data and IoT which require the refinement of images perceived by capture devices such as surveillance cameras or satellites. And for computer graphics, there is more and more realistic games, or realistic animations. Consequently, I chose options such as "Introduction to Computer Graphics and Image Processing" as early as my degree in order to specialise in the field. To pursue this goal, I decided to do my first internship in Montreal, Canada in digital imaging to confirm my professional project. In this internship oriented towards computer graphics, I first had to implement a translation function to an existing tool on an open source image editing software in C++ language. I also made an exchange year at Pazmany Peter Catholic University in Budapest, Hungary, an university member of the European Image processing and Computer Vision Master degree. And finally, I finished with my Master of Science in Computer Graphics and Image Analysis.

Today, my passion for computers and my taste for innovation and challenge is reflected in numerous participations in Hackathons on various subjects, such as video game development, software development or artificial intelligence. This also translates into the development of a video game for Android in my spare time. Check out the Suchiz Games tab for more informations on these !
